01 / PHILOSOPHY
"Inspired by the timeless principles of classical architecture and the needs of contemporary living."

We believe true architecture acts as a bridge between the ancient and the modern. Grounded in strength, precision, elegance, and timeless proportions, we create spaces that command respect, inspire tranquility, and endure as lasting legacies.

FOUNDER & PRINCIPAL ARCHITECT

Ar. D VINOD KUMAR

Principal Architect, Renaissance Studio
"Designing spaces with purpose, precision, and timeless character."

Ar. D Vinod Kumar leads Renaissance Studio with a passion for creating thoughtful, timeless, and functional spaces. His portfolio spans residential, commercial, hospitality, corporate, and interior design projects, each shaped by a commitment to design excellence and attention to detail.

Inspired by the enduring principles of classical architecture and contemporary design, he believes that great architecture balances aesthetics, purpose, and human experience.

04 / TIMELINE

The Design Process

Our approach transforms ideas into meaningful spaces through a structured and collaborative design journey.

01 / DISCOVER

Consultation & Briefing

Understanding your aspirations, lifestyle, site conditions, budget, and project requirements to establish a clear design direction.

02 / CONCEPTUALIZE

Design Development

Exploring spatial possibilities through sketches, plans, and design concepts that respond to the site, climate, and your vision.

03 / VISUALIZE

3D Views & Renderings

Bringing the design to life through realistic visualizations that help you experience the spaces before construction begins.

04 / DOCUMENT

Drawings & Approvals

Preparing detailed architectural, structural, and service drawings, along with statutory submissions and approvals as required.

05 / COORDINATE

Execution Support

Working closely with consultants, contractors, and vendors to ensure the design intent is accurately translated on site.

06 / DELIVER

Completion & Handover

Reviewing the finished project, ensuring quality and functionality, and handing over a space designed to endure and inspire.
